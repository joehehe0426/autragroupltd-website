Why Autra Group

Why Work With Us Directly?

Integrating with Wonder, KPay, or Yedpay directly means managing three relationships, three dashboards, and three support lines. We give you one.

One Point of Contact

When you work with Autra Group, you get a single relationship that covers every gateway. No finger-pointing between processors when something goes wrong — we resolve it.

  • Single onboarding process across all 7 APAC markets
  • One reconciliation report, one settlement schedule
  • Dedicated support in your local timezone — voice, chat, or email
  • We handle compliance, risk assessment, and gateway negotiations
  • Hardware sourcing, provisioning, and testing managed end-to-end

Deep Local Expertise

APAC payments are fragmented — different regulations, preferred methods, and settlement timelines in every market. We've spent years building the relationships and operational know-how that make multi-market acceptance possible without a dedicated in-house payments team.

  • 10+ years combined APAC payments experience
  • Licensed and compliant in all 7 operating markets
  • Existing relationships with Wonder, KPay, Yedpay — no cold onboarding
  • We speak the language — Cantonese, Mandarin, English, and local business practices
Our Service

Your Dedicated Payments Team

We handle the complexity of multi-gateway payments so you can focus on running your business.

01

Gateway Management

We manage all your gateway integrations — Wonder, KPay, and Yedpay — so you don't have to juggle terminals or reconcile separate dashboards. One relationship, every payment method.

02

Settlement & Reconciliation

We handle daily reconciliation across all gateways on your behalf. Fees, chargebacks, payouts — tracked and verified by our team. You get one clean report.

03

Rapid Onboarding

Our team handles KYC, terminal provisioning, and gateway activation for you — in hours, not weeks. Merchants transacting on day one.

04

APAC Coverage

Hardware Support
